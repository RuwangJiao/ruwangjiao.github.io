About Me

Ruwang Jiao received the PhD degree in School of Mechanical Engineering and Electronic Information, China University of Geosciences (Wuhan). He is now a postdoctoral research fellow in School of Engineering and Computer Science, Victoria University of Wellington, New Zealand.

Research Interest

  1. Evolutionary optimization:
    2.       constrained optimization, multi-objective optimization, Bayesian optimization
  2. Evolutionary learning:
    3.       transfer learning, feature selection in classification
  3. Real-world applications:
    4.       antenna deign, array synthesis
