About Me

Ruwang Jiao received the PhD degree in School of Mechanical Engineering and Electronic Information, China University of Geosciences (Wuhan). His research interest includes constrained optimization, multiobjective optimizaiton, surrogate-assisted expensive optimization, and their applications in antenna design.
